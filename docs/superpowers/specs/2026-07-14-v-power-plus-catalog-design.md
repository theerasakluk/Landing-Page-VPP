# V Power Plus Catalog Website Design

## Goal

Build a Thai-language catalog website for V Power Plus / วีพาวเวอร์พลัส that helps customers browse product categories and submit a quotation request.

## Approved Direction

Use the approved A + C mockup direction: a clean catalog structure inspired by Officework, combined with V Power Plus energy accents. The site should feel easy to scan, business-ready, and clearly branded with blue, green, and white.

## Audience

- General customers looking for office supplies, IT products, tools, furniture, and solar rooftop products.
- Corporate buyers who want to send a product list and receive a quotation.

## Primary User Flow

1. Customer lands on homepage and sees V Power Plus branding immediately.
2. Customer searches or scans the eight product categories.
3. Customer clicks "ขอใบเสนอราคา" or uses the quote form section.
4. Customer submits basic contact details and product requirements.

## Page Structure

- Header: transparent-background V Power Plus logo, search bar, category shortcut, quotation CTA.
- Navigation: Home, featured products, corporate customers, solar rooftop, contact.
- Hero: V Power Plus / วีพาวเวอร์พลัส headline, office supplies + IT + solar positioning, primary quote CTA, secondary category CTA.
- Proof strip: eight categories, corporate support, SKU help, solar-ready messaging.
- Category grid: eight requested categories with simple icons and clear labels.
- Quote section: short form-style area for name/company, phone/LINE, and request details.
- Footer: contact details, category links, quotation reminder.

## Product Categories

- อุปกรณ์สำนักงานเบ็ดเตล็ด
- อุปกรณ์สำนักงานอิเล็กทรอนิกส์
- เฟอร์นิเจอร์สำนักงาน
- อุปกรณ์และเครื่องมือ
- เครื่องดื่ม เครื่องใช้ และผลิตภัณฑ์อื่นๆ
- อุปกรณ์เครื่องเขียน และผลิตภัณฑ์กระดาษ
- ผลิตภัณฑ์สำหรับคอมพิวเตอร์และไอทีต่างๆ
- ผลิตภัณฑ์สำหรับโซลาร์รูฟท็อป

## Visual Style

- Palette: V Power blue, deep blue, energy green, aqua accents, white/light backgrounds.
- Layout: dense but clean catalog layout, not a marketing-only landing page.
- Logo: use the provided V Power Plus logo as the main brand asset and prepare a transparent-background version for the site.
- Imagery: use product-like visual blocks/icons rather than unrelated stock imagery for the first version.
- Components: compact header, search-first navigation, category tiles, quote CTA, business-oriented footer.

## Interactions

- Search bar is presentational in the first version unless product data is provided later.
- Category tiles are navigational anchors or static sections in the first version.
- Quote form is front-end only in the first version unless a destination email/API is provided later.

## Success Criteria

- The first viewport clearly says V Power Plus and shows that the site is a product catalog for quotation requests.
- The eight product categories are visible and readable on desktop and mobile.
- The quote request path is obvious from header, hero, and quote section.
- The site is responsive and does not clip Thai text on mobile.
